Family Friendly Kitchen

2277A Neptune Way, Comox

The kitchen is a nice showpiece, with granite tile backsplash, stainless steel appliances and an overhang counter area great for casual dining. There is plenty of counter space, and under cabinet lighting to give great light for working as well as ambient light during dinner parties.

There is also a large walk-in pantry so you can stock up on supplies for the family and keep them organized and close at hand.
